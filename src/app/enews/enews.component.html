2018 Upcoming MacBook

We examine when Apple is likely to update its MacBook for 2018, rounding up the rumours about the design, tech specs, new features and pricing All the evidence leads us to expect a major update to the MacBook in October. Not only could the price come down; we may also see a 13in screen option. We share everything we know about the 2018 MacBook below. Back in 2017 Apple updated the MacBook with Kaby Lake chips, and improved the integrated graphics. For the first time the MacBook was a powerful enough machine for us to recommend - despite the lack of ports and the high price. But that doesn't stop us wanting more from Apple's super-slim Mac laptop - and hopefully in 2018 our wishes will be granted.
